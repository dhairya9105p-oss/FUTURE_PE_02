## 🎯 Target Audience Matrix
The campaign strategy is mapped across three key demographics:
*   **College Students**: Focused on budget-friendliness, long battery life (42 hours) for long campus days, and lofi studying/gaming modes.
*   **Gym & Fitness Users**: Focused on secure locking fit, IPX4 sweatproof resistance, and boAt's Signature Bass sound to fuel workouts.
*   **Young Professionals**: Focused on dual microphone clarity with ENx noise cancellation for remote meetings, quick pairing (IWP), and sleek aesthetics.
